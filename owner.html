Thomas J. Pillstarch

Inventor - Philanthropist - Musician

Born in Croatia in 1976, Thomas Pillstarch quickly rose to fame as a prolific child prodigy. After receiving a Masters degree in communications from the University of Zagreb at the age of 15, he started his own business selling ice creams from his kitchen. He is now one of the worlds wealthiest men with an estimated net worth of over 40 Billion!
